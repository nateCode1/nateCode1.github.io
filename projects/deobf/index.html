De-Obfuscate

How to play & Game Info

The Story

In De-Obfuscate you play as windows defender anti-virus. The computer you're protecting has been attacked by a virus which has obfuscated the user's data. It is your job to figure out what order the data is meant to be in, then re-order it as fast as you can. The faster you fix everything the more stars you get, but be careful, there are traps left behind by the virus to stop you from fixing things.

Controls

Keyboard

Quick Restart - R

Jump - Space Bar / W

Move Right - D

Move Left - A

Go Back - Esc

Mouse

Use Buttons - Left Click


Objective

On each stage your objective is to figure out what order the circuts go in, then collect all the circuts in that order as fast as you can.
