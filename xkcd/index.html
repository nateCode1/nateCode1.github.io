Desktop Background Changer

Note that this site is NOT made by (nor affiliated with) Randall Munroe (yet). If you want to check out his work please go to xkcd.com.

Have you ever been staring at your desktop and said "I wish this was displaying the latest xkcd comic by renown author Randall Munroe"?

Well I doubt it, but if you're like me and love his work this may be for you anyways.

The xkcd background changer does exactly what it says on the tin: it automatically changes your desktop to be the latest, or a random xkcd comic.

Features!

The xkcd background changer boasts a number of features that will make you go: "is that it?". These features include and are limited to...

  • Automatically rendering at the resolution of your monitor (untested)
  • Lacking almost any quality assurance
  • Customizable colors
  • Displaying title AND alt text!
  • A very user friendly GUI
  • Only works on windows currently

Don't you want your desktop to look like this?

Install as .exe

You can install the xkcd desktop background changer as an executable file. Its portable, its quick, its easy. However, it does require you to trust that I am not distributing a virus. Which I am not by the way [Citation Needed].

Install with Python

You can install the xkcd desktop background changer by cloning the repo from my github, its a little more difficult than the exe, but it has the advantage of you being able to see exactly what it is your downloading. Please excuse my bad code.
